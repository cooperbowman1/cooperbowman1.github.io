I was born in 2003 in west central Indiana and grew up in a rural town of fewer than 400 people as one of five children. My interest in creativity and design came from my late father, Tim Bowman, who encouraged me from a young age.

In April of 2022, I joined Allen Monument Company at 18 years old. Over the past four years, I’ve grown into the lead role in the design department, where I’ve had the chance to bring new ideas into the monument industry while learning the importance of precision and quality.

In 2024, I started BOOPCOLLECTIVE©. This project focuses on designing and printing custom illustrated stickers. What began as a small idea has grown into a locally recognized brand that produces high-quality designs people can enjoy and share.

My work and my outlook are shaped by where I come from, the support of my family, and the lessons I’ve learned through both challenges and opportunities. I want what I create to be simple, lasting, and true to who I am.
