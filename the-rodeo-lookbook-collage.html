THE RODEO© LOOKBOOK COLLAGE

Two Lookbook Collages for THE RODEO©, a Tennessee-Based Clothing Brand. Made in collaboration with Amir Hefner.

NEW YORK SHOOT

This collage merges streetwear aesthetics with western motifs, creating a unique visual language for THE RODEO©'s Spring 2024 collection. The piece features layered textures, vintage rodeo imagery, and contemporary street style elements.

NASHVILLE SHOOT

Inspired by Tennessee's musical heritage, this collage blends retro concert visuals with modern fashion photography. The color palette reflects dusk in Nashville, with deep purples and oranges contrasting against the black and white archival images.
